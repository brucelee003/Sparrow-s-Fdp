Learning Goals

SESSION OBJECTIVES [v1.0]

By the end of this session, participants will master the following competencies.

Understand AI's Role

Grasp how AI functions as an intellectual partner in academic contexts.

Distinguish Approaches

Differentiate automation from augmentation in academic work.

Prompting Strategies

Learn effective techniques for engaging with AI systems.

Research Workflows

Explore AI-enabled processes for scholarly research and discovery.

Ethical Reflection

Consider ethical implications and responsible AI adoption practices.
